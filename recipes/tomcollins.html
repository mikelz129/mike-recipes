Tom Collins

An adult lemonade perfect for the summer.

Ingredients

  • Dry Gin - 2 oz.
  • Lemon Juice - 1 oz.
  • Simple Syrup - 1/4 oz.
  • Club Soda
  • Lemon wheel or maraschino cherry to garnish

Recipe

  1. Add the gin, lemon juice and simple syrup to a Collins glass.
  2. Fill with ice, top with club soda and stir.
  3. Garnish if desired.
  4. Enjoy!
