Daquiri

The classic Daiquiri is not an adult slushie.
It's a simple and sublime cocktail combining rum, lime and sugar.

Ingredients

  • Light Rum - 2 oz.
  • Lime Juice - 1 oz.
  • Simple Syrup - .75 oz.
  • Lime Wheel - Garnish

Recipe

  1. Add ingredients to a shaker with ice, and shake until well-chilled.
  2. Strain into a chilled coupe.
  3. Garnish with a lime twist.
  4. Enjoy!
