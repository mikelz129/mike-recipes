Gin and Tonic

The gin and tonic is the quintessential summer drink. Pour with your heart, not a jigger.

Ingredients

  • Gin - 1 heart pour
  • Tonic - 1 heart pour
  • Lime slice, if available

Recipe

  1. Pour gin into any container using your heart as a guide.
  2. Top with tonic.
  3. Add your lime slice if available.
  4. Enjoy!
