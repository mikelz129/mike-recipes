Old Fashioned

Easy to make, harder to top. The old fashioned is one of the oldest drinks on record and has remained a staple in the world of cocktails.

Ingredients

  • Whiskey of Choice - 2 oz.
  • Simple Syrup - 1/4 to 1/2 oz.
  • Angostura Bitters - 3-5 dashes
  • Maraschino Cherry
  • Orange peel for garnish

Recipe

  1. Combine whiskey, simple, and ango in a glass filled with ice.
  2. Use a bar spoon to stir.
  3. Strain over fresh ice or a large cube if available.
  4. Top with a cherry and a slice of orange. Drag the orange peel along the rim of the glass for additional flavor.
  5. Enjoy!
